The $x$-update in Theorem~\ref{thm:becr-repair} is identical to the one in
Theorem~\ref{thm:positive-epsilon-failure}. Therefore $x_t\to 0$
geometrically. Since $\epsilon_s>0$, this implies
\[
\phi(a x_t)\to 0.
\]
Because
\[
s_t=\operatorname{clip}\!\left(\phi(a x_t),s_{\min},s_{\max}\right)
\]
with $s_{\min}>0$, there exists $T_0<\infty$ such that
\[
s_t=s_{\min}\qquad\text{for all }t\ge T_0.
\]

For the raw-gradient residual branch, recall the raw-unit recursions
\[
h_t=b y_t+e_t,\qquad
z_t=\rho h_t,\qquad
e_{t+1}=h_t-z_t,\qquad
y_{t+1}=y_t-\eta s_t z_t.
\]
For every $t\ge T_0$, substituting $s_t=s_{\min}$ gives
\[
y_{t+1}
=
y_t-\eta s_{\min}\rho (b y_t+e_t)
=
(1-\eta b\rho s_{\min})y_t-\eta \rho s_{\min} e_t,
\]
and
\[
e_{t+1}
=
(1-\rho)(b y_t+e_t)
=
b(1-\rho)y_t+(1-\rho)e_t.
\]
Hence, for $t\ge T_0$,
\[
\begin{bmatrix}
y_{t+1}\\ e_{t+1}
\end{bmatrix}
=
A
\begin{bmatrix}
y_t\\ e_t
\end{bmatrix},
\qquad
A=
\begin{bmatrix}
1-\eta b\rho s_{\min} & -\eta \rho s_{\min}\\
b(1-\rho) & 1-\rho
\end{bmatrix}.
\]

The characteristic polynomial of $A$ is
\[
\lambda^2
-
\bigl(2-\rho(1+\eta b s_{\min})\bigr)\lambda
+
(1-\rho).
\]
Write it as $\lambda^2+a_1\lambda+a_0$ with
\[
a_1=-\bigl(2-\rho(1+\eta b s_{\min})\bigr),
\qquad
a_0=1-\rho.
\]
The Jury conditions for a monic quadratic are
\[
1+a_1+a_0>0,\qquad
1-a_1+a_0>0,\qquad
1-a_0>0.
\]
These become
\[
1+a_1+a_0=\eta b\rho s_{\min}>0,
\]
\[
1-a_1+a_0=4-2\rho-\eta b\rho s_{\min}>0,
\]
\[
1-a_0=\rho>0.
\]
The second inequality is equivalent to
\[
\rho<\frac{4}{2+\eta b s_{\min}}.
\]
Thus the assumption
\[
0<\rho<\frac{4}{2+\eta b s_{\min}}
\]
is exactly the Schur-stability condition for $A$.

Therefore there exist constants $M>0$ and $\gamma\in(0,1)$ such that
\[
\left\|
\begin{bmatrix}
y_t\\ e_t
\end{bmatrix}
\right\|
\le
M\gamma^{\,t-T_0}
\left\|
\begin{bmatrix}
y_{T_0}\\ e_{T_0}
\end{bmatrix}
\right\|
\qquad\text{for all }t\ge T_0.
\]
Hence $(y_t,e_t)\to(0,0)$.

Finally,
\[
\grad f(x_t,y_t)=(a x_t,b y_t)\to (0,0),
\]
so $\norm{\grad f(x_t,y_t)}\to 0$. Throughout the proof, the residual
variables $h_t$, $z_t$, and $e_t$ remain in raw-gradient units, while the
factor $s_t$ acts only in the parameter update. This proves
Theorem~\ref{thm:becr-repair}.
