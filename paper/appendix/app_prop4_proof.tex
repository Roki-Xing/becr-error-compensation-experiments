For an orthogonal projector $C(v)=PP^\top v$,
\[
v-\mathcal C_\rho(v)=(1-\rho)(I-C)v,
\]
so the squared error is
\[
\norm{v-\mathcal C_\rho(v)}^2=(1-\rho)^2\norm{(I-C)v}^2\le (1-\rho)^2\norm{v}^2.
\]
This is the contractive ingredient used by the \BECR-SGD abstraction.
